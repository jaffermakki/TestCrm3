Restore from Backup

Replaces products, customers, repairs, and settings with the contents of the backup file. Sales history (invoices) is left untouched. Staff PINs are not included in backups and must be re-set after a restore.

Danger Zone

Permanently deletes all products, customers, repairs, invoices, held carts, and cash-up history. Staff accounts, settings, and the audit log are kept. This cannot be undone.
